Red Portugal Douro Horta Osório Wines 14 12-18

The “H.O. – Horta Osório Wines” come exclusively from grapes produced on the farms of Casa Agrícola Horta Osório where, in addition to “old vineyards”, “new vineyards” were planted with the best traditional varieties of the Douro, in unique “terroirs”. The grapes are harvested when they have a perfect phenolic ripeness, which usually occurs on the 4th week of September. The entire harvest is done manually, which allows for a refined choice and selection. The transport of the grapes from the vineyard to the winery is done in small 20 kg containers. The entire winemaking process is accompanied, maintaining the fermentation at a controlled temperature, to ensure the high quality of the wines.
