Descrição Petrus is the biggest name in a star constellation of Bordeaux. Located on the right bank of Bordeaux in the small "Apellation" of "Pomerol", where the Merlot variety is queen. Until 1945 this wine was in the so-called "secret of the gods". In possession of Madame Loubat, which was joined by Jean Pierre Moueix. Wine began to be unveiled, whether or not it was the preferred wine of names like the Kennedys or Queen Elizabeth of England. Due to a relentless demand on the part of collectors and investors, combined with a very limited production, this wine is today one of the most expensive in the world. It is the wine that anyone would like to taste at least once in their life. A dream of wine.
