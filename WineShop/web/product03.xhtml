The Barca Velha is the epitome, the very superior pinnacle, the unquestionable symbol of the highest quality of Douro wines. Classic, intense, complex, elegant – these are the adjectives that describe what has been, since its creation in 1952, the most celebrated Portuguese wine. The Barca Velha is the foundation on which Casa Ferreirinha's reputation grew, the latter being the quality brand with the highest tradition in the Douro and one of its main world guides. Barca Velha is only declared in really exceptional years.
