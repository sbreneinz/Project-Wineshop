Pêra-Manca is the brand that Fundação Eugénio de Almeida uses for its exceptional wines. The red wines are produced from Trincadeira and Aragonez varieties. They are full-bodied, complex and elegant wines, with an aroma of raisins of fruits and essences from the aging woods. Due to the high quality of the tannins and woods used, these are wines that have great longevity, requiring some time to reveal their full potential.
