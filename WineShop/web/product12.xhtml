Monólogo Avesso P67 is a single variety wine produced at Quinta de Santa Teresa, from the vineyards of the P67 plot, using sustainable and organic farming practices. The Avesso variety is the native variety of Baião. Finding in this region its expression of excellence, with a climate of transition between the typical Atlantic climate of Vinho Verde and the continental climate of the Douro, this variety has the opportunity to reach, in a balanced way, complete levels of maturation, resulting in complex wines, delicate and long-lasting.
